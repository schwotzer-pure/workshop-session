Challenges for automotive manufacturers and suppliers and how we help you tackle them


Clear Direction in Times of Change
The automotive industry across Europe is facing profound change: shifting markets, geopolitical uncertainty and the pressure to transform through electrification, autonomous systems and digitalisation. In times like these, clarity and practical methods matter more than ever. With workshops like Headlines from the Future, 9 Spaces helps you create shared direction and align around a common vision so you can approach change proactively instead of simply reacting.

Collaboration Beyond Silos
Stronger collaboration across the value chain is equally essential. Yet silo thinking and outdated structures often stand in the way. Through joint workshops and clear role clarification tools such as Role Profiles, 9 Spaces helps you create transparency, alignment and better cooperation across teams and partners.

Strengthening Talent and Culture
At the same time, organisations need to attract, develop and retain talent while navigating cultural change. Tools like the Strengths Finder foster exchange, make strengths visible and strengthen team cohesion in order to build an environment where people can grow and contribute long term
