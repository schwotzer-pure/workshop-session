Feedback & conflicts: Turning tension into real change

Conflict can be a force for positive change. 9 Spaces helps you build an open feedback culture and use tension as a driver for change.

Book a demo

On this page, you’ll learn how to turn friction into growth with tools that strengthen teams.

We don’t have any problems here!

If you hear this sentence in your organisation, it’s worth paying attention. One thing is true: tension and friction don’t have a great reputation. They’re exhausting, and they slow things down. That’s true.

What’s also true is that tension arises wherever people work together. You can’t ban it, and you can only ignore it to a limited extent. It’s simply there.

We believe it makes sense to see tension as the engine of change and the fuel of an organisation. But to use this potential, you need good methods. Only then can tension be made visible, worked with, and translated into meaningful change.
