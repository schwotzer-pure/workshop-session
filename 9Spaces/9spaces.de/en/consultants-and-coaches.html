Ready-to-use workshops in minutes, not hours

On 9 Spaces you’ll find more than 150 ready-made workshops that you can start with minimal prep time.The workshops include various formats to support you in workshop facilitation:

  • Handouts you can distribute to your participants
  • Whiteboard templates (Miro & Conceptboard) you can duplicate or customise based on your needs
  • Facilitation guides with word-for-word examples you can use in your workshops
  • Tutorial videos that demonstrate each format
