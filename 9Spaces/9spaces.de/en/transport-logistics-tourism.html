The key challenges facing the transport industrye


  • Skills shortages: Many transport organisations are struggling to fill operational and specialist roles. Staff shortages place pressure on day-to-day operations and limit long-term growth.
  • Digital transformation under limited resources: While digitalisation is widely recognised as essential, organisations often lack the time, budget or internal expertise to implement meaningful change.
  • Sustainability as a competitive factor: Environmental responsibility and climate action are high on the agenda. However, many organisations find it difficult to embed sustainable practices into everyday processes in a practical and lasting way.


How 9 Spaces supports your organisation


  • More effective collaboration: Our workshop tools strengthen teams, reduce operational strain and help you make better use of existing resources.
  • Driving digital transformation: Clear structures, intuitive tools and ready-to-run workshops enable digital collaboration, even with limited budgets.
  • Embedding sustainable processes: With 9 Spaces, you develop sustainable ideas further, rethink processes with environmental impact in mind and engage your people in meaningful change.
